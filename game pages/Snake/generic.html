Snake
Unity, short game

Just another snake, but look at the pretty light!

Loading the game is around 15 seconds

Code

Use the arrow keys on the keyboard to change direction, collect the pink ribbons until none are left or play the endless mode to achieve new highscores. Collecting the ribbons adds links to the snake body and adds to the snakes speed.
