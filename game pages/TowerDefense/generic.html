Tower Defense
Unity, short game

3D PC, survive the endless waves of enemies by building towers to kill minions to gain money to build more towers

Loading the game is around 30 seconds

WebGL builds are not supported on mobile devices.
Tower Defense
Code

Use your money which apears in yellow on the top left of the screen to buy buy towers and place them around the map in strategic locations to gain most value. Right click dismisses the tower selected. Move around map with arrows or AWSD.
