SpaceX
Unity, short game

Mobile game, survive the endless waves of enemies in this 2D spaceship combat game

Loading the game is around 30 seconds

Code

Press on the upper part of the screen on any spot and drag the mouse in the desired direction to move. the bottom right joystick rotates the ship around its axis and the red button fires. the enemies increase every time you destroy all existing enemies on the map. they increase only to a certain amount but the waves continue.
